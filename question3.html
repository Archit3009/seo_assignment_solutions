Meta tags are an important part of a search engine optimization (SEO) strategy. They help search engines understand the content of a page, which can help provide more accurate search results. Meta tags can also help with:

  • Helping people understand your page: Meta tags help people understand the contents of your page.
  • Avoiding duplicate content: Meta tags can help avoid duplicate content issues.
  • Driving traffic: Meta tags can help drive clicks and organic search traffic.
  • Ranking for keywords: Meta tags can help your website rank for keywords relevant to your site.
  • Improving search rankings: Meta tags can help improve your search rankings and page visibility.
